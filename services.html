GOMASI

The Gomasi is a distinctive and significant traditional attire worn by Baganda woman in Uganda. It consists of a floor-length,brightly colored dress with a square neckline and short,puffed sleeves.The Gomasi is often made from silk or cotton fabric and is adorned with colorful patterns.
The Gomasi holds cultural and social importance for the Baganda people.It is not just a piece of clothing. It is worn mostlty on important ceremonies.
