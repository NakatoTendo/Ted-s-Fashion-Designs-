TED'S FASHION DESIGNS

Ted's fashion designs was established in 2020 by a strong woman of God "Nakato Tendo."It all began
when she discovered her passion for fashion and the desire to create her own fashion business during
her years in school.While persuing her certificate in Fashion Design at YMCA Comprehesive Institute,
she was exposed to a world of creativity,artistry and the intricacies of the fashion industry.It was
in those classrooms and design studios that her love for textiles,garment consructionand trend analysis
truly flourished.

Through her courseworks,internships and exposure to renowned fashion professors,her skills developed a
keen eye for design,and gained a deep understanding of the industry's in and outs.Her time in school
was not just earning a certificate;it was the transformative period that ignited her Entrepreneurial spirit

Inspired by the innovative projects and collaborations with fellow students"Kyomuhendo Moureen,Margie,angel
etc"realised that she had a unique vision to share with the world.Her experiences in school provided her
with the foundation,knowledge,and network necessary to turn her dream of lauching a fashion business in to
a reality. It was this educational journey that ultimately led her to found Ted's Fashion Designs where she
could fuse her passion with her Entrepreneurial drive to create a brand that reflects her unique style and
vision.

Ted's Fashion Designs has spent four years in field and we are looking forward to spend many years searving
people.

The name Ted's Fashion Designs was got from the founder's name"Tendo."
